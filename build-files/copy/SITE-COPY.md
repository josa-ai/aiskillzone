## NOTES FOR DEVELOPER

### Style & Format Guidelines

- **Headings:** Sentence case (only first word and proper nouns capitalized)
- **Contractions:** Use liberally (we're, you'll, don't, isn't, etc.)
- **Tone:** Warm, direct, conversational
- **Exclamation marks:** Rare (0–1 per page)
- **Lists:** Use bullet points for scanability
- **Color references:** Only for internal note (colors appear in design, not copy)
- **Platform names:** Never mention (Wix, WordPress, Squarespace, GoHighLevel, etc.)
- **Avoid:** "synergy," "leverage" (verb), "disrupt," "next-gen," "cutting-edge," "game-changer"
- **Oxford comma:** Always use (and, or, not &)

### Meta Tags
Every page section includes a meta title and meta description at the top for SEO purposes. Use these exactly as written.

### CTAs & Forms
- Primary discovery call CTA appears on: Homepage, About, Contact page, and bottom of all major service pages
- Form integration: All contact/booking forms should capture data to CRM
- Email collection: Newsletter signup on blog page and footer

### Local SEO
- Geographic targets mentioned naturally throughout: Lakeland, Winter Haven, Bartow, Haines City, Auburndale, Lake Wales, Tampa, St. Petersburg, Orlando
- "Central Florida" appears in taglines, headlines, and descriptions
- Location footer info included on all pages

### Service Pages Structure
All primary service pages (Website Design, Voice AI, AI Automation, AI Training) follow PAS framework:
1. **Problem:** What's broken?
2. **Agitation:** What's it costing you?
3. **Solution:** Here's how we fix it

Secondary service pages are shorter (no PAS structure) but still lead with customer benefit.

### Copywriting Frameworks Used
- **Homepage:** 21-Step Sales Letter (adapted for services site)
- **Service pages (primary):** PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)
- **About page:** Hook-Story-Offer
- **Secondary services:** Problem-first mini descriptions
